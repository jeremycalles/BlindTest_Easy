# Privacy Policy – BlindTest Easy

**Last updated:** March 2026  
**Developer:** Jérémy Calles  
**App:** BlindTest Easy (iOS)

This privacy policy describes how the app **BlindTest Easy** (“the app”) handles information when you use it.

---

## 1. Data we collect and how we use it

### 1.1 Data stored on your device only

The app stores the following data **only on your device** (using iOS storage). We do **not** send this data to our servers or to any third party.

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Player names** | The names you enter when setting up a game are saved so they can be suggested the next time you play. You can avoid this by not saving or by clearing the app data. |
| **Favorite playlists** | When you mark Deezer playlists as favorites, their identifiers and basic info (title, cover) are stored on your device so they appear in “Mes Blind Tests.” |

This data stays on your iPhone or iPad. Deleting the app removes it.

### 1.2 Data we do not collect

We do **not** collect:

- Name, email, or other account information (the app does not use accounts or sign-in).
- Precise location or device identifiers for tracking.
- Analytics, crash reports, or advertising-related data.

---

## 2. Third-party services

### 2.1 Deezer

The app uses the **Deezer API** to let you browse playlists and play music clips during the blind test. When you use these features:

- Your device sends requests to Deezer’s servers (e.g. to load charts, search playlists, or get track previews).
- We do **not** send your name, email, or any account data to Deezer; the app does not use a Deezer account.
- Deezer may receive technical data such as your device’s IP address. For how Deezer handles data, see [Deezer’s privacy policy](https://www.deezer.com/legal/cookies).

We do not control Deezer’s practices. Use of Deezer content is subject to [Deezer’s terms](https://www.deezer.com/legal/terms).

---

## 3. Data retention and deletion

- **On-device data:** Stored until you delete it (e.g. by removing the app or clearing app data in iOS Settings).
- We do **not** retain any of your data on our own servers.

---

## 4. Children

The app does not require an account or collect personal information. It is suitable for general audiences. If you believe a child has entered personal data in the app (e.g. in player names), you can remove it by deleting the app or clearing its data.

---

## 5. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
